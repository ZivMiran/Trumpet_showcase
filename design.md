# Trumpet Portfolio Site - Design & Motion Specification
**Target Aesthetic:** Awwwards-worthy, functional, high-end design case study.
**Purpose:** Sell the candidate (the designer) by showing a polished, professional, and thoughtful product showcase.

## 1. Visual Language
- **Background:** Charcoal Black (`#16171a`).
- **Primary Text:** Warm Off-White (`#f0ede5`).
- **Accent:** Brass Gold (`#e3b53a`).
- **Typography:** `Space Grotesk` (Google Fonts).

## 2. Motion (Restrained & Purposeful)
*Goal: Feel fast, sleek, and high-quality.*
- **Easing:** `cubic-bezier(0.25, 1, 0.5, 1)` (snappy).
- **Entrance:** Fade-in + slight Y-translate (20px).
- **Sticky Dynamics:** Editorial pattern (Section 3) – keep text fixed while the visualization scrolls past.

## 3. Presentation Standards
- **Borders:** "Flat Layout Borders" (1px solid, low opacity Brass Gold). Avoid heavy drop shadows.
- **Layout:** Desktop-first focus. Ensure responsive stacking on mobile.
- **Data Representation:** Use tabular lining numbers in stats to avoid layout shifting.
